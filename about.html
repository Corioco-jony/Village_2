About Village Garden


Village Garden Youth Organization is a community self help organization engaged in promoting community development through education. The organization offers free educational services to kids between the ages of 8 and 18 years as well as adults interested inlearning how to read and write or want to advance their education. In a community with an illitracy rate of 85%, educating the youth is imperative in achieving a a safe and healthy community.


The organization started from a humble beginning with four membersAlhaji Hussien Dilinja (Boosu) , Mohammed Amin Larry (Lion), Abdul Hamid Ayub (Zion) and Attah Seidu (Chief Builder). These four hard working people saw the need to offer their expertise to a community that was lossing its youth to crime and destruction. In a community where more than 95% of the youth are poor and illitrate, their only source of survival is to turn to crime.
